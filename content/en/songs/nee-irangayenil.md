## Pallavi

Nee Irangayenil Pughaledhu Amba\
Neela Jagannathan Marbil Uraidiru (urai thiru)

## Anupallavi

Thayirangavidil Seyuyir Vazhumo\
Sakala Ulagirkkum Nee Thaayallavo

## Charanam

Paarkkadalil Uditta Maniyae\
Paarkkadalil Uditta Thirumaniyae\
Saubhagya Lakshmi Ennaik-kadaikkaniyae\
Naark-kaviyum Pozhiyum Pulavorkkum\
Mei Gnaniyarkkum Uyar Vanavarkkum\
(Amba Nee Irangayenil...)
